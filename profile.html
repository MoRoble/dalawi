About Dalawi

DALAWI Consultancy and Training Services is a premier private consultancy firm established in 2018 by a dedicated group of education and development Somali professionals based in Mogadishu. Our mission is to provide innovative and effective consultancy and training solutions that drive sustainable development and educational excellence. We are driven by our desire to make meaningful contributions to the development arena, as well as by an imperative to be of service to our clients.

As a small but growing private company, we recognize that our survival depends on providing value for money and products that enhance our customers’ reputation and impact. To achieve our aims, we focus our work on a set range of specialties and strive to ensure that Dalawi complements and adds value to our customer’s development efforts. In the last six years we have developed four main technical specializations: project/program evaluations, Capacity building development trainings, research and baseline studies, institutional capacity and finance mechanisms and proposal development and project design.

Headquarters

DALAWI CONSULTANCY AND TRAINING SERVICES has an operation base in Mogadishu of Somalia with other offices in Baidoa, Kismayu and Galkacyo. We have recently established our Garowe office, with a diverse client base including NGOs, governments, donor agencies and the private sector.

Our Core Values

  • Accuracy: We do to the best of ability ensure that the data collected is as accurate as possible be it in baseline survey, independent monitoring or evaluations.
  • Credibility: We commit ourselves to collect data that is credible and based on reliable information extracted from respondents as well as general observations.
  • Confidentiality: Our policy advocates for respect of people’s right to provide information in confidence and make participants aware of the scope and limits of confidentiality.
  • Transparency: As an organization we believe in openness and our team clearly communicate to survey target populations the purpose of the assignments we undertake and the intended use of findings in all field work we do.
  • Honest Reporting: Our organization stands for honesty in all the work we do. It is our belief that only through honest reporting that our contributions become valuable to clients in terms of enhancement of quality of their programming and advocacy work.
  • Keeping of Timeline: Timing is of essence in all quality programming work. As an organization, we are cognizant of this fact and are always committed to keeping of deadlines in all contractual agreements.
  • Marching of Assignments with Professional Qualifications: In all the assignments we undertake, we ensure that it is only handled by people with expertise in the specific areas to ensure high quality work output.

Core Services

Educational Consultancy

Our educational consultancy services are designed to support and enhance the educational landscape in the Banadir Region. We offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the needs of students, educators, and institutions. Our key areas of focus include:

  • Educational Research: Our team conducts in-depth research on various educational topics to inform policy and practice. We specialize in analyzing the impact of different educational interventions and programs on student outcomes.
  • TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training): We offer consultancy services to develop and enhance TVET programs, ensuring they meet the needs of the local labor market and provide students with the skills required for employment.
  • School Feeding Programs: We provide expert advice on the implementation and management of school feeding programs to improve student enrollment and retention.
  • Curriculum Development: We assist schools in developing and updating their curricula to ensure they are relevant, comprehensive, and aligned with national standards.
  • Teacher Training: We provide professional development opportunities for teachers to enhance their instructional skills and stay updated with the latest educational practices.
  • Educational Technology: We advise on the integration of technology in the classroom to enhance teaching and learning experiences.
  • Policy Development: We support educational institutions and government bodies in developing and implementing effective educational policies.

Gender and Social Inclusion Training

Our training programs address issues of gender equality, social inclusion, and empowerment. We work with organizations to:

  • Conduct gender analyses and assessments
  • Develop and implement gender-responsive policies and strategies
  • Build the capacity of staff on gender-sensitive programming
  • Promote the inclusion of marginalized and vulnerable groups

Monitoring, Evaluation and Research Consultancy

We offer comprehensive monitoring, evaluation, and research services to track the progress and impact of development projects and programs. Our expertise includes:

  • Developing robust monitoring and evaluation frameworks
  • Conducting baseline studies, midterm, and end-line evaluations
  • Providing data collection, analysis, and reporting services
  • Designing and implementing research initiatives to inform decision-making

Peace Building and Good Governance Training

We provide training and capacity-building programs focused on promoting peace, conflict resolution, and good governance. Our services include:

  • Conflict analysis and mapping
  • Designing and facilitating peace-building workshops
  • Training on conflict-sensitive program implementation
  • Promoting inclusive and transparent governance practices

Staff Capacity Building and Customer Care Training

We offer tailored training programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of our clients' staff. Our focus areas include:

  • Leadership and management development
  • Effective communication and customer service
  • Team building and organizational culture
  • Technical and functional skills training
