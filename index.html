Overview

DALAWI Consultancy and Training Services is a premier private consultancy firm established in 2018 by a dedicated group of education and development Somali professionals based in Mogadishu. Our mission is to provide innovative and effective consultancy and training solutions that drive sustainable development and educational excellence. We are driven by our desire to make meaningful contributions to the development arena, as well as by an imperative to be of service to our clients. As a small but growing private company, we recognize that our survival depends on providing value for money and products that enhance our customers’ reputation and impact. To achieve our aims, we focus our work on a set range of specialties and strive to ensure that Dalawi complements and adds value to our customer’s development efforts. In the last six years we have developed four main technical specializations: project/program evaluations, Capacity building development trainings, research and baseline studies, institutional capacity and finance mechanisms and proposal development and project design.
